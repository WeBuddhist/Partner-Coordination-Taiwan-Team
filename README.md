# WeBuddhist: Vision & Content Partner Coordination

This repository serves as the central operational hub for the **Vision Partner and Content Partner Coordination** role. Our mission is to bridge the gap between WeBuddhist and the leaders and creators of the Chinese-speaking Buddhist world, with a primary focus on the Tibetan tradition in Taiwan.

---

## ☸️ Mission & North Star Metric

Performance in this role is evaluated solely by the growth of **Chinese-speaking Daily Active Buddhists (DABs)**.

- **DAB Definition:** Any Buddhist who practices, learns, or connects on the WeBuddhist apps on any given day.
- **The Single Metric Mandate:** All activities—from ecosystem mapping to partner onboarding—are valued based on their contribution to this single metric.

### 2026 Year-End Strategic Targets

- **Daily Active Users (DAU):** 10,000
- **Total Downloads/Users:** 100,000
- **Hybrid Success:** 4 Vision Partners successfully transitioned to hybrid (online/offline) community models.

## 📋 Operational Workflow & Responsibilities

### 1. Living Documents (Monthly Maintenance)

We maintain three shared reference documents to inform proactive campaigns:

- **Buddhist Groups in Taiwan:** A pipeline directory ranked by size and partner status.
- **Influential Buddhist Figures:** A list of prominent monastics and personalities ranked by audience size.
- **Buddhist Calendar:** A rolling calendar used to plan custom routines 4–6 weeks ahead of high-impact dates.

### 2. Phase 1: Vision Partners (2026)

Focusing on group leaders with a minimum of **1,000 followers**.

| Step | Action | Description |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Identify | Prospecting | Selecting groups from the Taiwan directory with existing routine activities. |
| Discover | Research | Using persona profiles, empathy maps, and value proposition canvases to find community pain points. |
| Prototype | Design | Drafting a one-page outline (title, cadence, texts) for partner review and iteration. |
| Onboard | Integration | Setting up group pages and choosing entry points (e.g., "365 Days with Shantideva"). |
| Maintain | Engagement | Holding monthly check-ins to review engagement data and resolve friction. |

### 3. Phase 2: Content Partners (2027)

Expanding to facilitate collaborations with Buddhist creators.

- **Facilitation:** Proposing structured plan outlines for creators to publish under their own name.
- **Integration:** Aligning creator outputs with the Buddhist Calendar and Vision Partner schedules to maximize DAB impact.

---

## 🎯 Partner Selection Criteria

We prioritize partners based on the following strategic criteria:

- **Influence:** Large existing following.
- **Consistency:** Maintains a regular group routine.
- **Mindset:** Open to modernization and digital growth.
- **Pain Point:** Seeking a more convenient way to retain and engage followers.
